Powerful Features

Everything you need to analyze Guild Wars GvG matches down to the finest detail.

Full 3D Map Replay

Replay GvG matches on fully rendered DirectX 11 maps with terrain, props, water, and sky. Navigate freely with orbit, pan, and zoom camera controls.

Picture-in-Picture Camera

Secondary viewport with independent camera controls. Watch one area of the map while keeping an eye on another — perfect for split analysis.

Auto Camera

Intelligent camera that follows the action. Configurable triggers: death events, low HP detection, lord targeting, flag carriers, resurrections, and isolated players.

Range Rings & Spirit Ranges

Team-colored range rings around agents visualizing skill ranges. Toggle individual ring types, spirit ranges for monks and ritualists. Dashed, solid, or filled styles.

Skill Lasers

Line-of-sight laser beams visualize targeted skills in real-time. See exactly who is casting what on whom during intense team fights, with fade-out animations.

Fog of War

Switch perspective — view from Blue team, Red team, ghost mode, or a single player's vision. Understand positioning and vision from any side of the battle.

Deep Analytics

Go beyond replay with analytical tools that reveal the strategy behind every match.

Flag Analytics & Timeline

Track every flag event — spawns, pickups, drops, and captures — on a visual timeline. Analyze flag stand control, split pressure, and morale expiry timing.

Piano Roll / Skill Timeline

Skill usage timeline per agent, laid out like a music score. Zoomable from 5s to 60s windows. See exact cast sequences, timing gaps, and coordination across the team.

Heatmaps

Position density visualization with multiple palettes — Thermal, Inferno, Viridis, team-specific, and dominance mode. Per-team or per-player tracking with adjustable opacity.

Combat Log

Filterable log covering damage, heals, skills, interrupts, cancels, deaths, blocks, and basic attacks. Skill name autocomplete, player filter, and time sync on row click.

Morale & Lord Damage

Track morale boosts with per-team timers (120s duration). Lord damage panel shows attacker breakdown, damage percentage, per-hit timeline, and phase counting.

Party Windows & Build Detection

Full team composition with profession icons, health bars, and conditions. Automatic build detection from profession counts and key skill matching via JSON definitions.

Additional Tools

Power features for data exploration and asset management.

Cloud Replay Browser

Browse, search, and filter match replays from cloud storage. Stream directly or cache locally for offline use. Background sync keeps your library current. Star ratings and match notes for organization.

DAT Browser

Explore the Guild Wars .dat archive — textures, models, audio, and binary data. Filter by type, ID, hash, or name. Export assets as PNG, DDS, OBJ, MP3, JSON, and TIFF.

3D Model Viewer

Interactive model preview with orbital camera, animation playback, wireframe mode, bone visualization, skinning toggle, and vertex color coding by bone index.

Spatial Audio

3D positional audio powered by BASS. Hear skill sounds from the direction they occur on the map. Per-agent skill cast and impact audio with cursor tracking.

Catapult & Bundle Tracking

Catapult lever states, repair kit and vine seed carry events on a dedicated timeline.

Rendering Options

Setup Wizard
